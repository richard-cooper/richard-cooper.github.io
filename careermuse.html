background

Career Muse is on a mission to inspire your career. Our Muses are hiring managers, career coaches, certified resume writers, and an intelligent machine named Camile, all dedicated to helping you write your career story.

problem

Tasked with researching and designing the interactions of Camile (an AI) to generate recommendations for rewrites and optimization of usersâ€™ resumes based on user data, NLP, and market analysis geared towards their ideal job title.

solution

Design the "Q&A" experience of Compose - a new UI built within the confines of Career Muse. Major goals included ensuring empathy is shared with the user every step of the way.

    • Cover Letter Writing

market analogs

    I combed for market analogs that were specifically not resume rewriting or career coaching. Examples of pages that went through a recent UX redesign that resulted in a delightful user experience. Focus on solving a difficult/painful task. Example: turbotax.com

  • Simplifies the complexity of filing taxes with natural language
  • Has unilateral navigation
  • Is personable, reassuring and encouraging
Time spent on Market Analogs: 1 day


Core Competencies Displayed: Bias for Action. Owning the Market Anaolog portion of research, it could have been easy over-invest time. Focusing on keeping to the timeboxed scheduled we had crafted during our team charter allowed to extract just-enough datat required to move on with the option of exploring further at a later date.

User Research analysis

      I conducted 6 user interviews using a standardized script that was written in a 30 minute group excerise. Script allowed for digging deeper into tangents revealed by the subject.

    • 90% of users are never satisfied with their resume - feels cold and technical
    • 55%  have multiple versions, which are edited for each job posting
    • Difficulty deciding on which content to use and where to place it within a resume

      • Time spent on User Research: 3.5 days + .5 day for User Research Analysis

      "The longer I wait to write a resume, the harder it is to start"

    Hypothesis: People are often motivated by desperation and frustration with their current job

empathy map

    I lead the creation of an Empathy Map to compile a proto-persona. I deligated team members to write on the board, review research and ensured we stayed on track with predetermined timeboxing slots.This excersise was instrumental in better understanding the creation of our two personas, Grace and Walter.

  • In order to compile a proto-persona, an Empathy Map was created based 100% from direct quotes from User Research
  • Focus on the WHY  behind Feelings, Tasks, Influences, Pain Points and Goals
  • Is personalable, reassuring and encouraging


    • Time spent on Empathy Map: .5 day

    Core Competencies Displayed: Dive Deep. Owning the Empathy Map creation demonstrated to the team that regardless of the size of the task, the amount of time spent on the task, or the glitz and glam of the task, I was willing to be involved on all levels.

Develop Voice & Tone Guide

I wrote, with Isabel Heyer, the entire Voice & Tone Guide.  Consideration for the following was paramount:

"People don't always REMEMBER what you say or even what you do, but they always remember how you made them feel." 

Guide includes context samples  for Core Values, how to write new statements, how to revise current copy, and a matrix of guidelines.
