background

Career Muse is on a mission to inspire your career. Our Muses are hiring managers, career coaches, certified resume writers, and an intelligent machine named Camile, all dedicated to helping you write your career story.

problem

Tasked with researching and designing the interactions of Camile (an AI) to generate recommendations for rewrites and optimization of usersâ€™ resumes based on user data, NLP, and market analysis geared towards their ideal job title.

solution

Design the "Q&A" experience of Compose - a new UI built within the confines of Career Muse. Major goals included ensuring empathy is shared with the user every step of the way.

market analogs
  • Simplifies the complexity of filing taxes with natural language
  • Has unilateral navigation
  • Is personable, reassuring and encouraging

User Research analysis
    • 90% of users are never satisfied with their resume - feels cold and technical
    • 55%  have multiple versions, which are edited for each job posting
    • Difficulty deciding on which content to use and where to place it within a resume

      • "The longer I wait to write a resume, the harder it is to start"

    Hypothesis: People are often motivated by desperation and frustration with their current job

  • Focus on the WHY  behind Feelings, Tasks, Influences, Pain Points and Goals
  • Is personalable, reassuring and encouraging

Develop Voice & Tone Guide

I wrote, with Isabel Heyer, the entire Voice & Tone Guide.  Consideration for the following was paramount:

"People don't always REMEMBER what you say or even what you do, but they always remember how you made them feel." 

Guide includes context samples  for Core Values, how to write new statements, how to revise current copy, and a matrix of guidelines.
